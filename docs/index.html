Remain MP random picker

This page is aimed at those of us who have a strongly-Leave MP who has not been responsive to our concerns regarding brexit. Under the circumstances when our own MP is not supportive on an issue, the parliament website suggests contacting other MPs who may have an interest. But which ones? There were so many MPs supporting Remain before the referendum that we may find it hard to select.

Although last year's referendum result was close to evenly split, our parliamentary boundaries mean that there are four times more Remain MPs in Leave constituencies, than there are Leave MPs in Remain constituencies. This puts Remain MPs disproportionately under pressure. We therefore suggest you may wish to contact four Remain MPs. Using the Click to Pick! button below will select four Remain MPs at random for you to contact.

Some Remain MPs will now have changed their position to support brexit, while others have maintained their original pro-European position. Both are contained in the list used by this page as we believe it is important to represent our concerns to both groups. Depending on your reason for writing, you may wish to research the MPs you select.

Click the button to pick at random four Remain-supporting MPs from the list.

The data used are derived from publically available sources such as www.parliament.uk and www.theyworkforyou.com. If you spot any errors please let us know at contact.dsgeorge@gmail.com.
